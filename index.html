Speak up for safety

Your voice matters!

  • Keep everyone safe – Report bullying, harassment, and unsafe situations.
  • Improve your environment – Broken facilities, damaged infrastructure.
  • Build a stronger community – Honest feedback helps students and teachers work together.
  • Be heard & encourage accountability – Your experiences and opinions matter.
  • Support mental health – Holding problems inside can be stressful; sharing concerns helps you feel supported.
  • Make school better for future students – Your actions today improve tomorrow’s experience for others.
  • Small reports can make big differences – Even minor issues can lead to meaningful change.
